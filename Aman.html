I attended school in the western suburbs of Melbourne graduating from year 12 from Lakeview Senior College. Whilst in highschool I was fortunate enough to complete a VET in schools course where I recived my Certificate III in Information Digital Media and Technology. I've been interested in IT from a very young age creating "backups" of games for my Nintendo DS' flashcart when I was 9 years old, to even building my own desktop at 14. My favourite hobby would definitely be video games and gaming, specifically playing MOBA's such as League of Legends, DoTA and HoTS. As of current I have no professional IT experience.
